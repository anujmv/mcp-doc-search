# Sample: Equipment Maintenance Notes

Sample document for grounded retrieval demos. Inspired by planned-maintenance workflows.

## Pump unit P-200
Service interval: 500 operating hours. Replace seal kit and inspect impeller for wear.
Common fault: cavitation noise indicates low suction pressure.

## Spare parts
- Seal kit SK-200
- Impeller IMP-200
- Bearing BRG-77

## Safety
Lock out and tag out before any service. Verify zero pressure before opening housing.
